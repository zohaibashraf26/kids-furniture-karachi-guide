# Kids Furniture in Karachi — Buyer's Guide 2026

A practical reference for parents in Karachi shopping for 
children's bedroom furniture.

## Bunk Beds

| Type | Price Range (PKR) |
|------|------------------|
| Standard bunk bed | 45,000 – 65,000 |
| With storage drawers | 65,000 – 100,000 |
| Premium / triple bunk | 100,000 – 175,000+ |

**What to check:** guardrail height (min 16cm above mattress), 
ladder width (min 28cm), solid wood frame vs MDF.

## Study Tables

| Type | Price Range (PKR) |
|------|------------------|
| Basic desk | 18,000 – 30,000 |
| With bookshelf & drawers | 30,000 – 50,000 |
| Premium ergonomic | 50,000 – 80,000 |

**What to check:** minimum 90cm surface width, adjustable 
height, banded edges (no sharp MDF corners).

## Kids Wardrobes

| Type | Price Range (PKR) |
|------|------------------|
| 2-door basic | 25,000 – 45,000 |
| 3-door with mirror | 45,000 – 75,000 |
| Custom built-in | 75,000 – 150,000+ |

**What to check:** slat spacing on interiors, drawer quality, 
sliding vs hinged doors for your room size.

## Buying Tips

- Measure doorways and ceiling height before ordering
- Ask for wood type in writing — solid wood outlasts MDF 
  significantly for bunk beds
- Confirm whether delivery includes assembly
- Ask for at least 1-year structural warranty
